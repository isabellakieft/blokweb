What is Moco Museum?

Step into the world of Moco. See iconic works from Warhol, Haring, Banksy, Basquiat, Kusama, and many more.

Discover one of Amsterdams most visited modern art museums showcasing contemporary, immersive, and digital art experiences, offering visitors a unique perspective on global and local artists.

Since opening its doors in 2016, this art museum in Amsterdam has become a cultural hotspot, showcasing iconic masterpieces from renowned modern and contemporary artists alongside cutting-edge works by emerging talents.

Get Tickets
Moco stands for Modern and Contemporary Museum in Amsterdam, where each visit offers a deep dive into creativity, reflection, and innovation. Moco Museum is located in the Villa Alsberg, a townhouse overlooking Museumplein in the heart of Amsterdam (between the Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum). Moco Museum’s space takeover of the Villa Alsberg is an act that goes hand in hand with our purpose: make modern art accessible to everyone and welcome all. From within Moco Museum, grows the mission and vision to express the endless power of art. A visit to Moco Museum puts you in the art center of Amsterdam with everything at your fingertips.

Modern Art Museum in Amsterdam

The modern contemporary museum of Amsterdam, Moco Museum, showcases the groundbreaking vision of iconic 20th-century artists who reshaped the art world.

Experience the collection at Moco Museum Amsterdam and discover why this modern art museum in Amsterdamis a highlight for visitors worldwide.

In the Modern Masters exhibition, visitors can experience Andy Warhol’s pop art, Jean-Michel Basquiat’s raw energy, Keith Haring’s socially conscious street-inspired artworks, and Yayoi Kusama’s polka-dot pieces. Celebrated for pushing boundaries and inspiring change, these masters transformed the way we see art and culture. Located in the heart of the city, this museum of modern art in Amsterdam offers an unmissable journey through vibrant creativity, making it a highlight for anyone exploring Amsterdam’s museum and art gallery scene.
